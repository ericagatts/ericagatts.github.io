About Me

I'm a human-centered product designer with 14 years of experience designing for healthcare and complex enterprise workflows. I've built my career on a simple belief: the harder a system is to understand, the more important good design becomes. I partner with clinical, product, and engineering teams to untangle complexity — surfacing the right information, at the right moment, for the people who need to act on it. Whether I'm designing for an oncologist interpreting genomic data or a care coordinator navigating a fragmented workflow, my goal is the same: make the hard thing feel effortless.
